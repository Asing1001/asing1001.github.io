To convert Markdown into Word format, [pandoc](https://github.com/jgm/pandoc/wiki) is the best tool I found.

## Step1 : [Install pandoc](https://github.com/jgm/pandoc/releases)
## Step2 : `pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.docx`
